The Soul of Valencia

A city where over 2,000 years of history seamlessly blend with avant-garde innovation. Discover the roots, traditions, and the radiant culture that define us.

public Deep Roots

Crossroads of Civilizations

Founded by the Romans in 138 BC as Valentia Edetanorum, our city has been shaped by centuries of Moorish rule followed by the Christian reconquest led by King James I of Aragon.

It is this unique collision of cultures that gave birth to our spectacular historic center, our intricate irrigation systems (like the Tribunal de las Aguas), and the profound Mediterranean character of our people.

La Cremà

On March 19th, all monuments are burned in a spectacular purification ritual, symbolizing spring's renewal and sweeping away the old.

Valencian: A Living Language

The heart of our identity lies in the Valencian language (Valencià). Spoken alongside Spanish, it's heard constantly in our markets, streets, and homes. It binds us to our poetry, music, and ancient literature, such as the chivalric romance Tirant lo Blanch.
