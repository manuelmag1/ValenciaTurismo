Traditional Dishes

From the world-famous Paella to our sweet Horchata. Discover the historic flavors that define our vivid and delicious Mediterranean cuisine.

Authentic Paella

Born in the fields of Albufera, true Valencian paella features rabbit, chicken, flat green beans, and garrofó beans slowly cooked over wood fire.

Fideuà de Gandia

A delightful seafood dish similar to paella but made with short noodles, bursting with the flavors of the Mediterranean sea.

Horchata & Fartons
