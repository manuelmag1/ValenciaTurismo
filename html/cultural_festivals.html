Cultural Festivals

Immerse yourself in our world-renowned celebrations of art, gunpowder, and history. We host the most vibrant and passionate festivals in all of Europe.

March 15 - 19

Las Fallas

Giant satirical monuments, thundering fireworks (mascletàs), stunning lights, and passionate parades culminate in a massive night of fire where all art is ceremoniously burnt.

August

La Tomatina

Held in the nearby town of Buñol, this is the world's largest food fight. Tens of thousands of participants throw over 100 metric tons of over-ripe tomatoes in the streets.
