Albufera Natural Park

Experience the freshwater lagoon where traditional rice culture meets diverse birdlife. A pristine ecosystem preserved for sustainability.

A Living Ecosystem

Where tradition meets sustainability

Freshwater Lagoon Ecosystem

Albufera is a 3,800-hectare freshwater lagoon surrounded by 7,800 hectares of rice fields. This unique ecosystem is crucial for migratory birds and home to traditional Valencian rice farming culture.

restaurant Local Dining

Albufera's El Palmar is famous for authentic paella made with local rice and fish from the lagoon. Over 50 traditional restaurants serve generations-old recipes.
