History & Heritage

Two millennia of history. From Roman foundations to the futuristic City of Arts and Sciences, explore the monumental legacy printed in our streets.

UNESCO World Heritage

La Lonja de la Seda

The Silk Exchange is a masterpiece of late Gothic architecture. Built between 1482 and 1533, its spectacular spiral columns in the main trading hall perfectly illustrate the wealth of Valencia's Golden Age.

13th Century Architecture

Valencia Cathedral

A brilliant mixture of Romanesque, French Gothic, Renaissance, Baroque and Neo-Classical styles. It also famously houses the Holy Chalice, widely revered as the true Holy Grail.
