Discover a hidden gem where pine forests meet Mediterranean dunes. A pristine coastal ecosystem preserved for future generations.

Pristine Coastal Ecosystem

The Dehesa del Saler is a unique natural reserve protecting 3,747 hectares of pine forests, dunes, and wetlands. This integral ecosystem showcases the Mediterranean's rare balance between sea and land vegetation.

Migratory Birds Paradise

A critical stopover for thousands of migratory birds. Spring and autumn bring spectacular flocks of warblers, herons, and raptors.

  • • 200+ bird species recorded
  • • Wetland dependent species
  • • Raptor hunting grounds
