Zero-Mile Dining

Valencia champions the 'Kilometer Zero' philosophy. Experience spectacular gastronomy crafted from ingredients sourced entirely from the local peri-urban orchards (La Huerta).

From Earth to Table

Surrounding our city is 'La Huerta', 120 square kilometers of fertile agricultural land that has fed our citizens since Roman times. This proximity allows our elite chefs to craft daily menus based strictly on morning harvests.
